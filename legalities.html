Legalities

This page explains the privacy practices of Omnimeta Global Solutions and describes how we protect information collected from our website about customers and users.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

  • We collect information that you provide when logging in to your account such as placing an order, subscribing to newsletters, or other requests for information.
  • Information received in connection with providing you service or fulfilling your order.
  • Like most websites, we use cookies to enhance user experience, gather general visitor information, and track visits to our website.

WHAT DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION FOR?

  • To improve customer service.
  • To process transactions.
  • To respond to your requests.
  • To send you emails containing information and updates pertaining to your order, notifying you of any product recall in addition to receiving occasional company news, updates, promotions, or related product or service information, etc.
  • To administer and fulfill a marketing promotion, survey or other site feature.
  • To share information with trusted third party companies, entities and individuals to perform functions on our behalf (i.e., credit card processing companies, order fulfillment and shipping, marketing and promotional distribution). However, these third parties may access your personal information on a confidential basis and only to the extent necessary to perform their functions, and all third party vendors must abide by our privacy policy.

HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?

  • We implement various security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information whenever you submit a request, place an order, or access your personal information. These security measures include: password protected directories and databases to safeguard your information or SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to ensure that your information is fully encrypted.
  • Although we have excellent security measures in place that meet industry standards, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

DO WE DISCLOSE ANY INFORMATION TO OUTSIDE PARTIES?

  • We do not sell, trade or transfer to outside parties your personal information without your consent. However, non identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising or other uses. We may use your information to address network integrity and security issues, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities or violations.
  • We will disclose personal information and/or an IP address, when required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary.

ONLINE PRIVACY POLICY ONLY

  • This Online Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our Site and not to any information collected offline.

YOUR CONSENT

  • By using our Site, you consent to our privacy policy.

HOW TO CONTACT US AND OPT OUT

  • If you have any questions or concerns about our online policy for this Site or its implementation, or you want to opt out of any subscriptions, you may email us at info@onimetaglobalsolutions.com

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

  • If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page, and/or update the Privacy Policy modification date below. Policy changes will apply only to information collected after the date of the change.
